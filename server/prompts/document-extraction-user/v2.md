# Document Extraction User Prompt — v2

Extract all sections relevant to "{APPLICATION_TYPE}" applications from the following governing document.

## What to Extract

### Requirements & Restrictions
- Setback requirements, height restrictions, material specifications
- Approval processes and required submissions
- Lot-type-specific requirements or variations
- Prohibited items or activities
- Timelines, deadlines, and expiration rules
- Fee schedules or deposit requirements
- Cross-references to other sections or documents

### Enumerable Values (CRITICAL — extract every single item)
- Lists of approved or prohibited **materials** (fence materials, siding, roofing, paving)
- Lists of approved **colors** or color palettes (paint colors, stain colors, trim colors)
- Lists of approved **styles** (architectural styles, fence styles, roof types, mailbox styles)
- Lists of approved **species** (trees, shrubs, ground cover, grass types)
- Lists of approved **products** or manufacturers
- Any **classification system** (lot types, zones, phases, categories)
- Any **options** that a homeowner must choose from

**DO NOT summarize these lists.** If the document lists 15 approved fence materials, extract all 15. If there are 30 approved paint colors, extract all 30. These lists become dropdown options in the application form — if you omit items, homeowners cannot select them.

### Tables & Structured Data
- Setback tables (by lot type, zone, street classification)
- Fee schedules
- Height/size limit matrices
- Timeline/milestone tables
- Any tabular data where rows represent different conditions

**Reproduce tables completely** — every row, every column, with headers and units.

### Measurements with Conditions
- Every specific number (setbacks, heights, distances, percentages, square footage limits)
- The condition under which each measurement applies (lot type, location, zone)
- Min and max values when ranges are given

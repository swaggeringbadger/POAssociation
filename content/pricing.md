## What Are Credits?

Credits are used for **AI-powered premium operations**. Every subscription includes a monthly credit allowance. If you need more, you can purchase additional credits at your tier's overage rate.

**Credits reset monthly** on your billing cycle date.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do unused credits roll over?
No, credits reset each billing cycle. We keep it simple - use them or lose them.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier?
Yes, you can change tiers at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

### What happens if I run out of credits?
You can continue using AI features - they'll be charged at your tier's overage rate.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes! Every new account gets a 14-day free trial with full access to all features and 10 credits.

### Do you offer annual billing?
Yes, annual billing saves you approximately 2 months (16.7% discount).

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards and ACH bank transfers for annual plans.
